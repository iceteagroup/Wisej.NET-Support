# Code of Conduct

## Purpose

This support forum exists to help users ask questions, report problems, request improvements, and share knowledge about building and deploying Wisej.NET applications.

To keep the forum useful, searchable, and welcoming, everyone is expected to follow these guidelines when opening issues, replying to others, or participating in discussions.

## Stay on Topic

Posts should be related to Wisej.NET support, including:

* Questions about building or deploying Wisej.NET applications
* Bug reports
* Feature requests
* Suggestions for improvements
* General how-to questions

Please post in English so moderators and other users can understand and respond effectively.

When asking for help, describe the problem clearly in words. Do not rely only on code snippets, screenshots, or private context.

## Prepare Before Posting

Before opening a new issue, please prepare enough information for others to understand and reproduce the problem.

When reporting a bug, include:

* A small, reproducible test case
* Sample data when relevant
* Your Wisej.NET version
* Your Visual Studio version
* Your .NET version
* Any relevant browser, operating system, or deployment details

Do not include generated build folders such as `/bin` or `/obj` in shared examples.

## Search First

Before opening a new issue, search existing issues and the documentation.

Your question may already have an answer, and duplicate posts make it harder for others to find useful information.

## Proofread Before Posting

Before submitting, re-read your post from the perspective of someone who does not know your application or situation.

Make sure your post:

* Explains the problem clearly
* Includes the necessary context
* Uses a descriptive title
* Provides steps to reproduce when possible
* Avoids unrelated details

Clear posts are easier to answer and more useful to future readers.

## Be Respectful

Treat everyone with kindness and patience.

Healthy disagreement is allowed, but personal attacks, insults, hostile language, or intentionally disruptive behavior are not.

If you see harmful or inappropriate content, flag it so a moderator can review it.

## Keep Secrets Out

Do not post confidential or sensitive information in this forum.

Never share:

* Passwords
* API keys
* License keys
* Access tokens
* Private customer data
* Confidential business information
* Sensitive screenshots or logs

Before posting code, logs, screenshots, configuration files, or sample data, remove or blur anything private.

## Zero Tolerance

The following behavior is not allowed:

* Harassment
* Discrimination
* Personal attacks
* Threats or intimidation
* Content that promotes harm, cruelty, abuse, or exploitation
* Posting confidential information without permission
* Repeated off-topic or unsuitable posts after being asked to stop

## Moderation and Consequences

Moderators may take action to keep the forum useful, respectful, and safe.

Possible actions include:

* Asking a user to clarify or edit a post
* Closing duplicate, incomplete, off-topic, or unsuitable issues
* Removing inappropriate comments
* Locking discussions
* Temporarily or permanently banning repeat offenders

Moderation decisions are made at the discretion of the forum moderators.

## Reporting Problems

If you see behavior that violates this Code of Conduct, please flag the content or contact a moderator.

Reports will be reviewed and handled appropriately.

## Goal

The goal of this forum is to help people solve problems, learn from each other, and build better Wisej.NET applications in a clear, respectful, and constructive environment.
